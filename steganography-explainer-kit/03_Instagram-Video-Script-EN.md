## Production notes

- **Two cuts:** 50s "explainer" (this) + a 15s "hook-only" teaser (shots 1–4 + CTA) for ads/Stories.
- **Captions:** burn in. ~70% of feed views are muted.
- **Safe zones:** keep text out of the top 220px / bottom 320px (IG UI covers it).
- **Hook variants to A/B test as shot 1:** (a) "this photo is hiding a secret"; (b) "your printer is a snitch"; (c) "you're already a spy and don't know it."
- **Music:** minimal dark synth + a clean "reveal" sparkle on every secret reveal. Keep beats on the cut points.
- **Accessibility:** the spectrogram/printer reveals should be shown visually, not just narrated.
- **The reveal motif:** every time a secret appears it glows **Signal Green (#3DDC84)**; everything ordinary stays muted. Consistency sells the idea.
