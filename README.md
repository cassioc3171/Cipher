# 🔐 Cipher - Encrypt messages with total private control

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Windows_Installer-blue)](https://github.com/cassioc3171/Cipher)

Cipher keeps your messages private. It uses AES-256-GCM encryption to protect your text. You create your own keys. Your messages stay on your device because the app works offline. There are no accounts and no servers. 

## 🛠️ How this app works

Cipher transforms your messages into scrambled code. You copy this code into any chat app or email program. The person you send it to needs Cipher and the same secret key to read your message. The software does not track your activity. It does not collect data. Your information stays inside your phone or computer.

## 🚀 Using the app for the first time

Open Cipher from your Start menu or desktop shortcut. The interface shows a main window with two tabs: Encrypt and Decrypt.

### To encrypt a message:

1. Select the Encrypt tab.
2. Type your secret password into the Key field. Use a strong password to ensure security. 
3. Type your message into the text box.
4. Click the Encrypt button.
5. The software turns your message into encrypted text on your screen.
6. Click the Copy button to capture the scrambled text.
7. Paste this text into your email or messaging service.

### To decrypt a message:

1. Copy the encrypted text from your chat window.
2. Open Cipher and select the Decrypt tab.
3. Type your secret password into the Key field.
4. Paste the scrambled text into the text box.
5. Click the Decrypt button.
6. The original message appears in the output window.

## 🛡️ Security features

- AES-256-GCM: This is a standard math method for locking data. It makes the message unreadable to everyone without your specific key.
- Offline-first: The app does not need the internet. It works inside your local memory.
- No telemetry: The software does not send usage reports or analytics to any company.
- Steganography support: You can hide secret messages inside plain images to disguise your communications.

## 📋 Troubleshooting

If the application does not open, check the following:

- Restart your computer. Windows updates sometimes block new software from launching on the first attempt.
- Ensure your user account allows the installation of local programs.
- Check if your antivirus program identifies the file as unknown. Since this app works offline and handles encryption, some security programs flag it as a precaution. Choose the option to run the file anyway.

## 🌐 Privacy policy

Cipher does not connect to a server. There is no central database. We cannot see your messages. We cannot reset your password. If you lose your password, you lose access to the encrypted data. Write your passwords in a safe place.

## 📖 Glossary

- Encrypt: To scramble a message so only someone with the right key can read it.
- Decrypt: To unscramble a message back to its original form.
- Key: A secret password used to lock or unlock your private messages.
- Steganography: The process of hiding a secret file inside another file, like a photo.
- Offline: Acting without a connection to the internet. 

## ⚙️ Advanced settings

You can change how the app looks in the settings menu. You can switch between light mode and dark mode. You can also clear your local history. Clearing history removes any recent messages stored temporarily in the app workspace. This creates an extra layer of privacy if someone else uses your computer. 

Always close the app when you finish your work. This clears the active keys from your computer memory. If you leave the app open, your passwords stay in the internal memory until you close the program or shut down your machine. Keep your computer locked when you leave your desk.
